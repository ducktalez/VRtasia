# Ideas

> Raw brainstorm. No structure required. Promote items to [docs/implementation-plan.md](docs/implementation-plan.md) once they become real tasks.

## Core direction
- VR zombie shooter, single-player first
- Three-developer team, all VR-dev newcomers
- Heavy use of AI coding agents

## Gameplay seeds
- Pistol holstered on belt — physical draw to start combat
- Wave-based survival in a small arena (warehouse? church? subway?)
- Limited ammo → forces reload mechanic (magazine swap with off-hand)
- Headshots count more, body shots stagger
- Melee fallback: shove zombies back when they get too close
- Slow-motion when ducking? (comfort risk — evaluate)

## Atmosphere
- Dim lighting, flashlight on weak hand
- Diegetic UI (ammo counter on gun slide, no HUD)
- Sound design > visuals for tension

## Stretch / later
- Co-op (2–3 players) — needs backend, deferred
- Procedural arena layout
- Boss zombie types
- Pickup weapons (shotgun, SMG)

## Things to explicitly avoid (for now)
- Open world
- Story cutscenes
- Inventory UI menus inside VR
- Anything requiring custom networking before single-player works
